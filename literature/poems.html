Howl by Allen Ginsberg
Unlike most other poems here, I did not read this one in the Writer's Almanac. I read it first in Moe's Books in Berkeley, then later in City Lights Bookstore in San Francisco. Then, every subsequent time I saw it anywhere. Then I bought a used copy at my local bookstore.

Journey of the Magi by T.S. Eliot
Originally published in the Almanac on December 24, 2013. I first read this in English class in senior year. At the time, I had no strong feelings toward it. But when it popped up as the daily poem in December and I read it again, I loved it.
